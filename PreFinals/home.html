AI Definition

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as reasoning, learning, decision-making, and perception.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a generative artificial intelligence chatbot developed by OpenAI. The "GPT" in its name stands for "Generative Pre-trained Transformer," which describes the underlying technology that processes and responds to prompts.

DeepSeek is a Chinese AI company that develops and releases open-source large language models (LLMs).

Gemini

Gemini AI is a family of multimodal artificial intelligence models created by Google and developed by Google DeepMind that can understand and generate various types of data, including text, images, audio, and video.
