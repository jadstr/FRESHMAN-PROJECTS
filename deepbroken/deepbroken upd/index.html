About attunements

Deepwoken is a challenging and immersive RPG experience set in a dark fantasy world. Players embark on perilous journeys, battling formidable foes and uncovering hidden secrets. With its intricate lore, dynamic combat system, and vast open world, Deepwoken offers an unforgettable adventure for those brave enough to face its trials.

Description

Deepwoken is a difficult game with permanent character loss. Losing characters is a part of the game that should be expected. Each death is a lesson learned, and with each new character, you grow stronger and wiser. Embrace the challenge, adapt your strategies, and rise from the ashes of your past failures to conquer the world of Lumen.

In Deepwoken, every story is yours to tell. Though, however short they may be is up to you. Arm yourself with a selection of hundreds of unique abilities. Plunder and forge a vast array of weapons, armor, and equipment. Fight and overcome the odds, clawing your way back from every defeat.

Explore Lumen, a fragmented world that spans hostile waters, bustling towns, ruined sanctuaries, and nightmarish geometries. Delve through a variety of challenging dungeons, and test your might in PvP.

Uncover the mysteries of a dying world with hundreds of characters and an in-game glossary that expands with every new piece of lore uncovered.

Every aspect of your playthrough can be customised with a range of origins, attributes, modifiers, upgrades, and a diverse amount of customisation options.
